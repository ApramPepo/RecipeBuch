Cookies Recipe

Making bakery-worthy chocolate chip cookies is much easier than it seems. You'll find a detailed ingredient list and step-by-step instructions in the recipe below;

Ingredients

  • Butter
  • Eggs
  • Water
  • Vanila
  • Baking Soda
  • Salt
  • Flour
  • Chocolate Chips

Steps

  1. Step 1: Beat the butter and sugars, then beat in the eggs and vanilla.
  2. Step 2: Dissolve the baking soda in hot water and add to the mixture.
  3. Step 3: Stir in the flour, chocolate chips.
  4. Step 4: Drop dough onto a prepared baking sheet.
  5. Step 5: Bake until the edges are golden brown.

Enjoy your cookies!
